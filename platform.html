— ABOUT US —

Empowering Migrants with Clarity, Honesty & Trust

The Global Council for Migration Awareness and Social Welfare (GCMA) is a dedicated organisation committed to safeguarding the rights, dignity, and well-being of migrants worldwide. We stand at the frontlines of migration advocacy — educating communities, exposing fraudulent practices, and championing ethical migration processes.

GCMA believes that every individual's journey — whether crossing borders for work, education, or a better life — deserves to be guided by accurate information, transparent procedures, and unwavering integrity.

Exam Tips

  • Read each question carefully — look for keywords like "first", "priority", and "most important"
  • For SATA, treat each option independently — it's either right or wrong on its own
  • Use the calculator for dosage questions; never calculate in your head under pressure
  • Eliminate obviously wrong options first, then choose from what remains
  • Apply ABC (Airway, Breathing, Circulation) and Maslow's hierarchy for prioritisation
