What is NursePrep Pro?

Built by Nurses,
Designed to Get You Licensed

NursePrep Pro is a comprehensive NCLEX exam preparation platform created specifically for nursing students and graduates preparing for the NCLEX-RN and NCLEX-PN licensure examinations.

We combine expert-recorded video lectures, thousands of practice questions aligned to the NCLEX Clinical Judgment Measurement Model (CJMM), and AI-powered performance analytics — all in one clean, focused platform.
